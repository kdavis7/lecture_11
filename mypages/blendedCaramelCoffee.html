BLENDED CARAMEL COFFEE RECIPE
Brew time: 10 minutes | Yield: Makes one cup
Ingredients:
  • Ground Coffee Beans
  • Hot Water
  • 1 Ceramic Cup and 1 spoon
  • 1 Coffee Timer
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar(optional)
Steps:
  • Boil Water
  • Brew Coffee.
  • Combine Water and Coffee into the cup.
  • Mix together with a spoon.
  • Add sugar as needed.
